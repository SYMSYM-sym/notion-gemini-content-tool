Do NOT hardcode any brand-specific content in generation prompts.

**Why:** The app should work for any Notion database, not just the Her Health Info account. Previous versions had @herhealthinfo, sage green/blush pink brand colors, and "women's health and wellness" baked into every prompt.

**How to apply:** All prompts should follow the visual direction from the Notion entry exactly. The content type determines the prompt structure (photo vs graphic vs carousel), but brand details come from the Notion data, not from hardcoded strings.
